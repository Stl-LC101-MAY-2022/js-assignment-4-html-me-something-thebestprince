Haruki Murakami

Few have captured the world through literature the way Haruki Murakami has. Murakami is known for his unconventional approach to fiction. And is regarded as a master of "magic realism". A craft of fictional writing which I describe as fantastical and or unordianry events in the seeming mundane or ordianary. Murakami's characters often seem like everyday ordinary people. Sometimes that may even come off as boring or uniteresting. But as you turn the pages, and peel back the layers of Murakami's narratives, you see that his characters and narratives are anything but normal some are even like super heroes. Murakami's work taught me that reality and fantasy can be two sides of the same coin and both aspects need each other to exist.
